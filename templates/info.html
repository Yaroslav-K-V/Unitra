A short practical overview of what Unitra is, how to use it, and what stays local on your machine.

What Unitra is

Unitra is a local-first Python test tool for solo developers. It helps you draft tests quickly in Quick and work through calmer repository flows in Workspace.

Quick vs Workspace

Quick is for snippets, single files, and fast one-off local work.
Workspace is for repositories, managed test files, review, reruns, and longer-lived workflows.

Local-first notes

Workspace metadata is stored locally in .unitra.
Your API key stays in .env.
Most flows are local-first; fallback model settings only matter when AI assistance is enabled and used.
